The map displayed on this page is illustrative of the "cartographic gaze." It projects the God's-eye view in that all the continents are visible at once in this two-dimensional depiction, a view that is not possible in the natural world. The god in the God's-eye view ofthe cartographic gaze is not any sort of supreme being. Rather it is the cartographer himself. It is his vision of the world that the rest of us see.
